Foundation refresh + per-component review. shadcn-style structural conventions, Linear-grade polish, signal-orange accent, IBM Plex stack. Both themes first-class.

Foundations

Colors are OKLCH-driven, so light and dark themes share a single perceptual model. Neutrals carry a warm cast in light, a cool cast in dark — both stay legible at small sizes. The accent is the only saturated colour in the chrome; status colours are reserved for status meaning.
