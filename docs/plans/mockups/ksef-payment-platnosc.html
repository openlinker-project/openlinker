Bank account, SWIFT, payment term, and skonto fields collapse away — they don't apply without a Przelew-family method. Payment term and skonto stay independently settable regardless of method in the real implementation (a Gotówka connection can still declare a default term), but the mockup groups them under Przelew here since that's the common case.
